how to learn how to code

In our modern day and age, software development is becoming more and more important as society leans upon technology and AI. Software development and maintaining is incredibly important, and you can start helping too! What's recommended first is to look up tutorials and resources online. One of our personal favorites is w3schools, which is a very helpful site not only for learning the entirety of many languages but also just simple elements.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is developing rapidly, which is a large concern for many. However, many software developers aren't worried about AI taking their jobs, and, they actually embrace it. AI and devolepment can go hand-in-hand, with ai providing basic code and shortening time-consuming tasks leaving teams to be able to focus on other, more important tasks. Even though you're not working for a big tech company, AI is still your best friend. It can provide code, help with debugging, all sorts of wonderous things.
