To make a molotov cocktail, all that is required is a glass bottle, gasoline, perhaps some lighter fluid (optional) , a rag, and a lighter. Pour the gasoline and lighter fuel into the bottle, insert the rag, and you're ready to go. Light up the rag & throw at any unsuspecting invaders of your hometown.
